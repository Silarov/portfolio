About Me


Hi! My name is Severin Probst. I'm a 17-year-old photographer based in a small village in Switzerland. I started photographing all the way back in 2019, where I used my mom's camera. I didn't have a clue what I was doing, but I immediately fell in love with photography. I started learning all the rules for composition, lighting, and color and started to get slowly better at it. Now, after over 3 years of pursuing this hobby, I can comfortably say that I know more or less what I'm doing. When I'm not behind a camera, I love listening to and making music. I play the electric guitar and bass and got started on learning saxophone.

If you have any questions regarding me or my work, feel free to write me an email to "severinprobst@gmx.ch".
